Who am I?

"Your friendly ex-firefighter turned web developer"

Hey! I'm Kevon Senior, a Web Developer and Designer with a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites and digital experiences.

Before entering this world of web development, I previously served as a Firefighter in my home country for approximately 7 years. This experience taught me the importance of teamwork, quick thinking, and staying calm under pressure.

My goal is to leverage these skills in the tech industry to build innovative solutions that make a difference.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading about design trends, or enjoying a good cup of tea.

I believe in continuous learning and strive to improve my skills every day. I'm excited about the future of web development and can't wait to see where this journey takes me.
